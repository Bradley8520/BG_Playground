Taxonomy Revision Handoff Document
Overview
This document outlines proposed changes to the VOC (Voice of Customer) taxonomy for Topics and Sub-Topics. The goal is to guide another LLM in implementing these taxonomy modifications.

Proposed Changes by Theme

1. Password/Passcode
* Current Category: PLATFORM
* Current Topic: Account Access & Login
* Current Sub-Topic: "Password Reset / Verification Too Difficult"
* Proposed Change: Add passcode reset as part of the theme. Perhaps change Sub-Topic name to “Password/Passcode Reset”. Password can apply to login for email app or AT&T account login (app or website). Passcode can apply to verification of customer (rep asks for customer’s passcode) or part of a multi-factor authentication or password reset. Review whether we should have separate sub-topics for password/passcode reset and customer verification.

2. Privacy
* Current Category: "Part of Account Management subtopics, but see where it could fall under POLICY"
* Proposed Change: Consider moving to POLICY category; possible Sub-Topic name could be “Data Privacy/Security”.

3. AT&T Phone
* Current Category: ?
* Proposed Change: Add a Sub-Topic “Home Phone” somewhere in the taxonomy, make recommendation of Category and Topic. Home Phone should include variations of a home phone to include landline (POTS), VOIP, and AT&T Phone – Advanced. AT&T Phone – Advanced is a wireless home phone service (uses VOICP technology over AT&T wireless network) designed as a landline replacement to move customers off copper; but may also be the VOIP device.

4. Bill Inquiry and General Bill
* Current Category: PRICING
* Current Topic: ?
* Proposed Change: Bill Inquiry is a call intent, but I expect much of Bill Inquiry calls do get captured in one of billing-related themes. About 0.7% of all verbatims hit current Bill Inquiry theme; another 0.8% hit General Bill (supposed to be mutually exclusive from other billing sub-topics; but some co-occurrence may occur). Help me think through capturing Bill Inquiry or General Bill mentions, but do they have any specificity to provide additional context or actionability.

5. Discounts
* Current Category: PRICING
* Current Topic: Promotions & Discounts
* Proposed Change: Add new sub-topics for:
* Signature Savings Program: AT&T offers up to a 20% discount on our most popular unlimited wireless plans for eligible business employees (corporate discount program), union members, and students.
* Appreciation Savings Program: AT&T offers up to a 20% discount on our most popular unlimited wireless plans may be available for eligible military, veterans, teachers, retired responders, and their families.
* Access by AT&T (low cost Internet): Access from AT&T is a low-cost home internet program designed to help eligible households stay connected with reliable AT&T service at an affordable monthly price.
* Wireless + Fiber Bundle Discount: 
* 55+ Wireless + Internet Bundle Discount

6. Call Audio Issues
* Current Category: PROCESS
* Proposed Change: Create "Call Center Environment & Atmosphere" topic (parallel to Retail's "Store Environment & Atmosphere") with sub-topics:
* Difficulty hearing (headset issues)
* Loud background noise (crowded call centers)

7. Perceived Trust-Wrong Information
* Current Category: PROCESS
* Current Topic: Perceived Trust-Wrong Information
* Proposed Change: Move the following sub-topics from PEOPLE>Rep Knowledge & Accuracy:
* Rep Was Dishonest / Misleading / Deceptive; relabel sub-topic “Perceived Trust”
* Inconsistent Information
* Create 3rd sub-topic “Wrong Information”

8. System Outage
* Current Category: PLATFORM
* Proposed Change:
* Make "In-Store Technology & Systems" channel-neutral (not just Retail)
* "Transaction Was Smooth/System Worked Well" should be channel-neutral
* Add sub-topic for Store WiFi for Retail channel
* Add sub-topic for Pay Kiosks for Retail channel
* Change "Store Systems Were Slow/Caused Delays" for Retail (OPUS/tablets) to be channel-neutral; changing sub-topic name to “System Slow/Outage caused Delays”. These applies to the systems or tools reps use to assist customers. Customer verbatims likely mention computer, tablet, system, tool was down or not working. This does not include Store WiFi or Pay Kiosks.


9. Wait, Holds, & Speed of Service (or Interaction Timeliness)
* Current Category: PROCESS
* Current Topic: Wait Time & Speed of Service
* Proposed Change: Break out into separate sub-topics:
* Long Wait for Rep
* Long/Multiple Holds
* Service Was Fast/Quick/Efficient
* Overall Interaction Took Too Long

10. Rep Communication
* Current Category: PEOPLE
* Current Topic: Rename Rep Communication Clarity to “Rep Communication”
* Status: change the existing sub-topics from four to five:
* Language Barrier or Comprehension Issue 
* Request for Different Communication Support 
* Rep Communicated Clearly & Effectively 
* Unclear, Incomplete, or Confusing Communication 
* Poor Listening or Interruptive Behavior

11. Cancellation & Switching Intent
* Current Category: POLICY
* Current Topic: Cancellation & Switching Intent
* Proposed Change: Split Switching to Competitor – Xfinity / Spectrum / Other ISP; Separate Xfinity (Comcast) from Spectrum (Charter); don’t need other ISP

12. Device & Equipment
* Current Category: PRODUCT
* Proposed Change: Add sub-topics:
* Modem-Router
* Residential Gateway
* WiFi Extender
* Setup/Activate Equipment

13. General Customer Service
* Current Category: PROCESS
* Topic: Create General Customer Service topic
* Proposed Change: Keep separate from "General Rep Quality" - add as distinct taxonomy item:
* Positive General Customer Service
* Negative General Customer Service

14. Retail Store
* Current Category: PROCESS
* Topic: Create topic “Cross-Channel Friction”
* Proposed Change: Create the following sub-topics:
* Care-to-Store Referral: Tech / Device Issue
* Care-to-Store Referral: Billing, Account & Policy
* Store-to-Care Deflection
* Circular Runaround: Neither Channel Resolves
* Conflicting Information: Store vs. Care


15. Shop & Purchase
* Current Category: PROCESS
* Topic: Shop & Purchase
* Sub-Topics: Create the following sub-topics:
* New Account
* Activation & Porting
* Add a Line
* Upgrade
* Trade-In
* Fulfillment - Order
* Fulfillment - Delivery
* Returns
* Exchange & Replace
* Warranty & Device Protection Exchange
* Unauthorized Account Changes
